Course Overview

Technology for Tomorrow

The Bachelor of Information Technology (BIT) at TIIS develops broad and coherent knowledge and practical skills in ICT. You'll gain expertise across software development, networking, web and mobile technologies, and cyber security.

17 core subjects build a comprehensive IT foundation, while 7 specialisation units let you focus your career direction — choose Web & Mobile Applications Development or Cyber Security.

✓Demonstrate broad knowledge of IT systems and technologies
✓Design, develop, and deploy software and IT solutions
✓Apply cybersecurity concepts and ethical frameworks in IT practice
✓Work independently and collaboratively in professional contexts
