Course Overview

Technology for Tomorrow

The Bachelor of Information Technology (BIT) at TIIS provides a comprehensive education in modern IT practice. From programming and database management to networking, web development, and information systems, this degree equips you with the practical and theoretical knowledge to thrive in the technology sector.

The curriculum is designed to be hands-on and industry-relevant, ensuring graduates can contribute to organisations from day one. You'll work on real-world projects and develop a portfolio of work that demonstrates your capabilities to future employers.

Key Learning Outcomes

  • ✓ Design, develop, and deploy software solutions
  • ✓ Manage databases and information systems
  • ✓ Apply cybersecurity concepts and best practices
  • ✓ Solve complex problems using computational thinking
Curriculum

Course Structure

Year 1 — Foundations

Programming Fundamentals, Web Technologies, Computer Systems, Database Design, Discrete Mathematics, and IT Professional Practice.

Year 2 — Development

Data Structures & Algorithms, Networking, Systems Analysis & Design, Software Engineering, Cybersecurity Fundamentals, and Operating Systems.

Year 3 — Specialisation

Cloud Computing, Mobile Development, AI & Machine Learning, Project Management, and a capstone IT project.
