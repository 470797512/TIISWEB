## 1. Course Overview

The Master of Professional Accounting (Advanced) (MPAA) at The Institute of International Studies (TIIS) is a postgraduate qualification designed to develop advanced accounting knowledge, analytical capability, and professional expertise for dynamic global business environments.

The program integrates:

- core accounting disciplines

- advanced professional accounting units

- strategic leadership capability

to ensure graduates are industry-ready, technically competent, and capable of operating at a senior professional level.

This program has been developed in response to strong industry demand for accounting professionals with advanced technical, analytical, and professional skills.

Course Details

- AQF Level: 9 (Masters Degree – Coursework)

- CRICOS Code: 102564D

- Duration: 2 years full-time (or part-time equivalent) \*

- <span id="_Toc227486145" class="anchor"></span>Delivery Mode: Face-to-Face, Online and Hybrid (Blended)\*\*

- Campus: Sydney & Melbourne

- Intakes: Multiple intakes per year

\* Duration may vary depending on enrolment load and progression.  
\*\* Delivery modes are subject to regulatory approval, course availability, and visa conditions.

## 2. Course Structure

The MPAA consists of 16 compulsory units, structured to progressively develop:

- foundational business and accounting knowledge

- core accounting and systems capability

- advanced professional accounting expertise

- strategic leadership and decision-making skills

4\. Learning Outcomes

Graduates of the MPAA will be able to:

- Apply advanced accounting knowledge in complex organisational contexts

- Analyse, interpret, and evaluate financial and business data

- Develop solutions to complex accounting and regulatory problems

- Demonstrate leadership, communication, and professional judgement

- Critically evaluate contemporary issues impacting the accounting profession

## 7. Entry Requirements

Academic Requirements

Applicants must have:

- A recognised undergraduate degree (non-accounting discipline), OR

- A relevant graduate certificate or equivalent qualification

⚠️ Note:  
Applicants with an Australian undergraduate degree in accounting are not eligible for the MPAA.

English Language Requirements

International students must demonstrate:

- IELTS 6.5 overall (no band below 6.0) or equivalent

Students not meeting requirements may complete an ELICOS program prior to commencement.
