## 1. Course Overview

The Bachelor of Business (BBus) at The Institute of International Studies (TIIS) is an undergraduate qualification designed to provide broad and coherent knowledge across key business disciplines, combined with practical skills for professional practice.

The program prepares graduates to:

- understand core business functions including management, accounting, marketing, and economics

- analyse and solve business problems in dynamic environments

- develop professional communication, teamwork, and leadership skills

- pursue careers across a wide range of industries

The course integrates foundation knowledge, specialisation pathways, and applied learning, ensuring graduates are industry-ready and adaptable to evolving business environments.

## 2. Course Details

## 4. Course Structure

The BBus consists of 24 units, structured as:

- 8 Core Business Units (Foundation)

- 8 Specialisation Units (Major)

- 8 Additional Units (Specialisation / Capstone / Electives)

Students may:

- complete a specialisation, OR

- follow a general business pathway

## 6. Learning Outcomes

Graduates of the BBus will be able to:

- Demonstrate broad knowledge of business and management disciplines

- Analyse and solve complex business problems

- Apply critical thinking and data-informed decision-making

- Communicate effectively in professional environments

- Work independently and collaboratively

- Apply ethical and responsible business practices

## 7. Delivery Mode and Study Requirements

Delivery Modes

- Face-to-Face: On-campus structured classes

- Online: LMS-supported learning including virtual delivery

- Hybrid: Combination of on-campus and online learning

All delivery modes ensure:

- equivalent learning outcomes

- consistent assessment standards

- appropriate academic support

⚠️ International Students:  
Delivery must comply with visa conditions under the ESOS Act and National Code.

Technology Requirements

Students are expected to have access to:

- A modern computer or laptop

- A reliable internet connection

- Ability to use business and data analysis software tools

## 8. Entry Requirements

Academic Requirements

Applicants must have:

- Completion of Year 12 (or equivalent), OR

- AQF Level 5 qualification or higher, OR

- Approved pathway program

English Language Requirements

- IELTS 6.0 overall (no band below 6.0), or equivalent
