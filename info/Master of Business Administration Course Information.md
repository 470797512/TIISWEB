## 1. Course Overview

The Master of Business Administration (MBA) is a postgraduate qualification designed to develop advanced business knowledge and leadership capabilities for a global business environment.

- AQF Level: 9 (Masters Degree – Coursework)

- CRICOS Code: 107617M

- Duration: 2 years full-time (or part-time equivalent)\*

- Delivery Mode: Face-to-Face, Online and Hybrid (blended delivery)\*\*

- Campus Locations: Sydney and Melbourne

- Intakes: Multiple intakes per year

\* Duration may vary depending on enrolment load and progression.  
\*\* Delivery modes are subject to regulatory approval, course availability, and visa conditions.

## 3. Course Structure

The MBA consists of 16 subjects, including core subjects and a capstone project.

## 4. Subject Structure

## 7. Entry Requirements
