Course Overview

Elevate Your Financial Career

The Graduate Diploma of Financial Planning (GDFP) is designed to equip you with the skills and knowledge needed to excel in the financial planning industry. This program prepares you to provide quality financial advice to individuals and businesses, meeting the education requirements for financial advisers as set by FASEA (Financial Adviser Standards and Ethics Authority).

Through eight units covering financial planning, investment management, insurance, superannuation, tax planning, and ethics, you'll graduate ready to sit the financial adviser exam and begin your career in this rewarding profession.

Key Learning Outcomes

  • ✓ Develop and present comprehensive financial plans
  • ✓ Understand investment strategies and risk management
  • ✓ Navigate the regulatory and ethical framework for financial advice
  • ✓ Meet FASEA education requirements for financial advisers
