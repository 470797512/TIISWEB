Program Overview

Stay Compliant,
Stay Current

All relevant providers (except provisional relevant providers) must complete a minimum of 40 hours of CPD each year, as mandated by the Corporations (Relevant Providers Continuing Professional Development Standard) Determination 2018.

At least 70% of the applicable minimum hours must be spent on qualifying CPD activities approved by the relevant provider's authorising AFS licensee. The TIIS Online CPD program is designed to cover all mandatory categories and provide a clear, structured pathway to meeting your annual obligations.

Part-time advisers may reduce to a minimum of 36 hours with prior written consent from their authorising AFS licensee.

Mandatory Categories

CPD Requirements Breakdown

The Minister has set minimum hours across four mandatory categories. Our program covers all of them.

5 hrs
Technical Competence

Stay up-to-date with financial products, strategies, and technical knowledge required for quality advice.

5 hrs
Client Care & Practice

Enhance client engagement, communication skills, and practice management capabilities.

5 hrs
Regulatory Compliance

Understand regulatory changes, consumer protection obligations, and compliance requirements.

9 hrs
Professionalism & Ethics

Develop professional judgement, ethical reasoning, and understand your obligations as a financial adviser.

The balance of up to 40 hours must consist of qualifying CPD activities. For qualified tax relevant providers, an additional 5 hours of CPD in tax (financial) advice is required.

Who This Is For

Designed for Financial Advisers

Relevant Providers

Financial advisers authorised to provide personal advice to retail clients under an AFS licence.

Tax (Financial) Advisers

Qualified tax relevant providers who provide tax (financial) advice services — including the additional 5-hour tax CPD requirement.

Practice Managers

Compliance managers and practice leaders seeking to maintain their professional standing.

AFS Licensees

Licensees can use TIIS Online CPD as a structured program for their authorised representatives.

Your Obligations

Record Keeping & Compliance

Complete Records

Maintain complete and accurate records of all CPD activities throughout the year.

Licensee Access

Be prepared to provide copies of your CPD records to your AFS licensee upon request.

Register Updates

AFS licensees must update the Financial Advisers Register if CPD requirements are not met.
