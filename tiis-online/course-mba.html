Program Overview

Lead with Confidence

The TIIS Online MBA is a comprehensive postgraduate program designed to develop strategic leaders. Through a combination of contemporary business theory and practical application, you'll gain the skills to lead teams, drive innovation, and make impactful business decisions.

Our MBA curriculum is designed in consultation with industry partners to ensure relevance across sectors including finance, technology, healthcare, consulting, and entrepreneurship. You'll study alongside a diverse cohort of professionals from across Australia, enriching your learning through shared experience.

Key Learning Outcomes

  • ✓ Formulate and execute strategic business plans
  • ✓ Lead diverse teams and manage organisational change
  • ✓ Analyse financial performance and make data-driven decisions
  • ✓ Develop innovative solutions to complex business challenges
  • ✓ Navigate ethical dimensions of business leadership
Curriculum

Program Structure

12 units across four core pillars of business leadership, studied over 18 months (full-time) or 36 months (part-time).

Leadership & Strategy

Strategic Management, Organisational Leadership, Innovation & Entrepreneurship.

Finance & Economics

Corporate Finance, Managerial Economics, Financial Analysis & Reporting.

Marketing & Operations

Strategic Marketing, Supply Chain Management, Business Analytics.

People & Governance

Human Resource Management, Corporate Governance, Business Ethics & Law.

Admissions

Entry Requirements

Academic

A recognised bachelor's degree (or equivalent) in any discipline.

Work Experience

Minimum 2 years of professional work experience is preferred but not mandatory.

English

IELTS overall 6.5 (no band below 6.0), or equivalent.

Delivery

100% online. Study from anywhere in Australia at your own pace.

Intakes

Jan, Mar, May, Jul, Sep, Nov

Fees

$2,500 per unit (12 units). FEE-HELP available for eligible domestic students.
