Course Overview

Lead with Confidence

The MBA at TIIS is a postgraduate qualification designed to develop advanced business knowledge and leadership capabilities for a global business environment. It combines 10 core units, a Business Capstone Project, and a 5-unit specialisation.

✓Apply advanced business knowledge in complex organisational contexts
