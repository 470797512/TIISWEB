Course Overview

Lead with Confidence

The Master of Business Administration (MBA) at TIIS is a comprehensive postgraduate program designed for aspiring and current managers who want to elevate their leadership capabilities and business acumen. The program covers strategic management, innovation, leadership, and global business perspectives.

Through a combination of rigorous academic study and practical application, you'll develop the competencies required to lead organisations through complex challenges and to drive innovation and sustainable growth.

Key Learning Outcomes

  • ✓ Lead and manage organisations in complex global environments
  • ✓ Apply strategic thinking and innovation to business challenges
  • ✓ Develop advanced analytical and decision-making skills
  • ✓ Integrate knowledge across business functions for holistic leadership
Curriculum

Program Structure

Core Units

Strategic Management, Leadership & People, Business Analytics, Managerial Economics, Corporate Governance & Ethics, and Marketing Management.

Specialisation Units

Innovation & Entrepreneurship, International Business, Financial Management, and Project Management.

Capstone

An applied business capstone project integrating all learning into a real-world strategic challenge.

Admissions

Entry Requirements

Academic Requirements

A recognised bachelor's degree, OR a GCBA from TIIS (with full credit transfer), OR equivalent professional experience.

English Language

IELTS overall 6.5 (no band below 6.0), or equivalent.

Intakes & Location

Jan, Mar, May, Jul, Sep, Nov — Sydney & Melbourne
