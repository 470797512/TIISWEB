1

Gather Your Documentation

Prepare and attach all required documents with your application according to TIIS entry requirements.

2

Submit Your Application

Complete the application forms and send them to TIIS via email at info@tiis.edu.au.

3

Receive Your Offer

4

Pay & Enrol

Secure your place by paying tuition fees. You'll receive a Confirmation of Enrolment (COE).

Entry Requirements

Admission Criteria

TIIS maintains clear and fair admission criteria to ensure students are well-prepared for their chosen program of study. Requirements vary depending on the level and discipline of your course.

Undergraduate Programs

  • • Completion of Australian Year 12 or equivalent international qualification
  • • English language proficiency (IELTS 6.0 or equivalent)
  • • Additional requirements as specified per course

Postgraduate Programs

  • • A recognised bachelor's degree or equivalent
  • • English language proficiency (IELTS 6.5 or equivalent)
  • • Relevant work experience may be considered for certain programs

Payment Information

Tuition fees are due prior to the commencement of each study period. TIIS offers flexible payment options to accommodate your financial situation.

Upon fee payment, you will receive a Confirmation of Enrolment (COE), which is required for your visa application (international students).

For detailed fee information for specific courses, please refer to our fee schedule or contact our admissions team.
