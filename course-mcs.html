Course Overview

Defend the Digital World

The MCS at TIIS develops advanced knowledge, technical capability, and professional expertise in cyber security. It integrates foundation IT knowledge, core cyber security disciplines, business governance capability, and a capstone project.

✓Apply advanced cyber security knowledge to design and manage secure systems
✓Analyse and evaluate cyber security risks, threats, and governance frameworks
✓Develop technical and strategic solutions to cyber security challenges
✓Apply ethical, regulatory, and global perspectives in cyber security practice

Pathway Into This Program

Graduates of the Graduate Certificate in Information Technology (GCIT) may enter the MCS with full credit transfer for the 4 completed GCIT units.

Entry Requirements

Academic Requirements

A recognised bachelor's degree (any discipline), Graduate Certificate/Diploma, or equivalent. GCIT graduates enter with full credit transfer.

English Language

IELTS 6.5 overall (no band below 6.0), or equivalent.

Technology Requirements

High-performance computer (min. 16GB RAM recommended), reliable high-speed internet, ability to run virtualisation and cyber security tools.
