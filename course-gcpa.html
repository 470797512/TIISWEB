Course Overview

Begin Your Accounting Journey

The GCPA provides foundational accounting knowledge and practical financial skills for entry-level professional practice. It is designed as a standalone qualification or as a structured pathway into the Master of Professional Accounting Advanced (MPAA).

✓Demonstrate understanding of core accounting, legal, and financial principles
✓Analyse financial data and apply accounting concepts to business problems
✓Interpret and communicate financial information effectively
✓Pathway directly into the MPAA with full credit transfer
