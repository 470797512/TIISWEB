Course Overview

Begin Your Accounting Journey

The Graduate Certificate in Professional Accounting (GCPA) provides a foundation in accounting principles for graduates from non-accounting backgrounds. This 6-month program is the perfect entry point into the world of professional accounting and serves as a direct pathway to the Master of Professional Accounting Advanced (MPAA) at TIIS.

You'll develop competencies in financial accounting, management accounting, business law, and economics — the core knowledge areas that underpin successful accounting careers.

Key Learning Outcomes

  • ✓ Understand fundamental accounting theories and practices
  • ✓ Apply accounting concepts in business decision-making
  • ✓ Develop analytical and quantitative skills
  • ✓ Pathway directly into the MPAA with credit transfer
