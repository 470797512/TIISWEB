Course Overview

Build Your Business Foundation

The Bachelor of Business (BBus) at TIIS equips you with a thorough understanding of contemporary business practices and management principles. This program provides the theoretical knowledge and practical skills needed to thrive in a competitive global marketplace.

Through a blend of core business units and specialised electives, you'll gain expertise across key areas including management, marketing, finance, entrepreneurship, and strategic thinking. The degree is designed to develop well-rounded graduates ready to contribute from day one in the workforce.

Key Learning Outcomes

  • ✓ Apply business theories and frameworks to real-world scenarios
  • ✓ Demonstrate leadership and teamwork in organisational settings
  • ✓ Analyse and solve complex business problems ethically
  • ✓ Communicate effectively across diverse cultural contexts
Curriculum

Course Structure

24 units across core business disciplines and elective specialisations.

Year 1 — Foundations

Build your base with units in accounting principles, economics, business communications, management, and marketing fundamentals.

Year 2 — Development

Deepen your knowledge with business law, organisational behaviour, finance, statistics, and strategic management.

Year 3 — Specialisation

Focus on your interests with electives in entrepreneurship, international business, digital marketing, and a capstone project.
