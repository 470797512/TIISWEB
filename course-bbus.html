Course Overview

Build Your Business Foundation

The Bachelor of Business (BBus) at TIIS is an undergraduate qualification designed to provide broad and coherent knowledge across key business disciplines, combined with practical skills for professional practice.

Through 8 core business units and a chosen specialisation stream, you'll gain expertise across management, marketing, finance, entrepreneurship and strategic thinking — emerging as an industry-ready, adaptable graduate.

✓Demonstrate broad knowledge of business and management disciplines
✓Analyse and solve complex business problems using critical thinking
✓Communicate effectively in diverse professional environments
✓Apply ethical and responsible business practices
