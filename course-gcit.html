Course Overview

Your IT Postgraduate Pathway

The Graduate Certificate in Information Technology (GCIT) provides foundational postgraduate IT knowledge for graduates transitioning into the technology sector. This focused 6-month program covers essential IT concepts and serves as a direct pathway into the Master of Cyber Security (MCS) at TIIS.

You'll study core areas including IT infrastructure, programming concepts, database systems, and an introduction to cyber security – establishing the groundwork for further specialisation in advanced IT fields.

Key Learning Outcomes

  • ✓ Understand core information technology concepts
  • ✓ Apply IT fundamentals to solve practical problems
  • ✓ Develop foundational cyber security awareness
  • ✓ Pathway into the Master of Cyber Security with credit transfer
