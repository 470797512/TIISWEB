Course Overview

Your Gateway to the MBA

The Graduate Certificate in Business Administration (GCBA) is an intensive 6-month program designed for graduates and professionals seeking to develop foundational skills in business management and leadership. It serves as a direct pathway into the TIIS Master of Business Administration (MBA).

Through four carefully selected units, you'll gain an understanding of contemporary management theory, organisational behaviour, and strategic decision-making — preparing you for more advanced postgraduate study or enhanced career opportunities.

Key Learning Outcomes

  • ✓ Understand foundational business administration concepts
  • ✓ Apply management principles in organisational contexts
  • ✓ Develop strategic thinking and leadership capabilities
  • ✓ Pathway directly into the MBA with credit transfer
