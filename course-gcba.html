Course Overview

Your Gateway to the MBA

The GCBA provides foundational business knowledge and practical management skills. Ideal for professionals seeking to upskill, newly promoted managers, and students intending to pathway into the MBA program.

The GCBA structure aligns directly with the first stage of the MBA — ensuring a smooth transition with full credit transfer available.

✓Demonstrate understanding of accounting, economics, marketing, and management
✓Apply management theories in business contexts
✓Identify and implement solutions to business challenges
✓Pathway directly into the MBA with full credit transfer
