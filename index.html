How to Apply

Getting started at TIIS is straightforward. Follow these four simple steps.

1

Gather Documents

Prepare all necessary documents as per TIIS entry requirements.

2

Submit Application

Complete the application forms and send them to TIIS via email.

3

Receive Offer

If you meet our requirements, TIIS will issue a Letter of Offer.

4

Pay & Enrol

Pay tuition fees to receive your Confirmation of Enrolment (COE).
