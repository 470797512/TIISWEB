Course Overview

Advanced. Professional. Accredited.

The MPAA at TIIS develops advanced accounting knowledge, analytical capability, and professional expertise for dynamic global business environments. The program integrates core accounting disciplines, advanced professional accounting units, and strategic leadership capability.

✓Apply advanced accounting knowledge in complex organisational contexts
✓Analyse, interpret, and evaluate financial and business data
✓Demonstrate leadership, communication, and professional judgement
✓Critically evaluate contemporary issues impacting the accounting profession
Eligibility Note

Applicants with an Australian undergraduate degree in accounting are not eligible. The MPAA is designed for graduates from non-accounting disciplines.

Pathway Into This Program

Graduates of the Graduate Certificate in Professional Accounting (GCPA) may enter the MPAA with full credit transfer for the 4 completed units, entering directly at Stage 2.
