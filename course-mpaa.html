Course Overview

Accredited. Industry-Ready.

The Master of Professional Accounting Advanced (MPAA) is TIIS's flagship accounting program, accredited by CPA Australia, Chartered Accountants Australia and New Zealand (CAANZ), and ACCA (the Association of Chartered Certified Accountants). This two-year program provides comprehensive training across all areas of professional accounting practice.

Whether you're transitioning into accounting from another field or deepening your existing expertise, this program equips you with the technical knowledge, ethical understanding, and professional skills to succeed in a rapidly evolving accounting landscape.

Accreditations

  • ★ CPA Australia — meets all accreditation requirements
  • ★ CAANZ — recognised for chartered accountant pathway
  • ★ ACCA — accredited for professional membership
Curriculum

Program Structure

16 units across core accounting, advanced practice, and professional skills.

Core Accounting

Financial Accounting, Management Accounting, Auditing & Assurance, Business Law, Economics, Corporate Finance, Taxation Law, and Business Statistics.

Advanced Practice

Advanced Financial Reporting, Strategic Management Accounting, Advanced Auditing, International Accounting, and Accounting Information Systems.

Professional Skills

Ethics & Governance, Research Methods, and a capstone project integrating accounting knowledge into practice.
