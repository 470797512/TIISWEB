Welcome to TIIS

For Today, For Tomorrow

At The Institute of International Studies (TIIS), we extend our heartfelt gratitude to you for choosing us as your higher education destination. As an accredited provider (CRICOS Provider No: 03705J, Provider No: PRV14085), TIIS delivers a transformative and enriching learning journey.

We firmly believe that your selected courses mark the first significant step towards realising your career aspirations. Our programs are meticulously designed to align with industry needs — ensuring education serves as a direct bridge to your future career.

Our Commitment

Industry-Relevant Education

Upon completing your degree at TIIS, you'll emerge equipped with the essential tools and knowledge needed to confidently enter the workforce. TIIS actively connects its courses to industry-accredited standards wherever feasible.

Graduates of our Master of Professional Accounting program become eligible for accreditation with CPA Australia, Chartered Accountants Australian and New Zealand (CAANZ), and ACCA. These strong ties ensure you enter the global job market well-prepared.

Our Journey

A Decade of Growth

2015
TIIS Founded

Established by visionary shareholders to create a unique educational platform for career-ready skills and enriching life experiences.

2017
TEQSA Accreditation

Received formal accreditation from the Australian Government's Tertiary Education Quality and Standards Agency.

2018
Professional Accounting Recognised

Master of Professional Accounting earned accreditation from CPA Australia, CAANZ, and ACCA.

2022
Business Programs Launched

Introduced the Bachelor of Business (June) and Master of Business Administration (September).

2023
Bachelor of IT Introduced

Launched the Bachelor of Information Technology, staying at the forefront of technological advancement.

2024
Cyber Security & Financial Planning

Approved to offer Master of Cyber Security, Graduate Certificate in IT, and Graduate Diploma of Financial Planning — our first domestic and online offering.
